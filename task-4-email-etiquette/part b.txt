#Do’s
1.Think before you post – make sure your content is respectful and appropriate.
2.Protect your privacy – adjust settings and avoid sharing personal details like address or phone number.
3.Verify information before sharing – avoid spreading misinformation or fake news.
4.Be respectful in conversations – maintain a polite tone even during disagreements.
5.Build a positive digital footprint – share achievements, skills, and constructive content.

#Don’ts
1.Don’t post offensive or harmful content – including hate speech, bullying, or inappropriate jokes.
2.Don’t overshare personal information – it can lead to privacy and safety risks.
3.Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling – it can damage your reputation.
4.Don’t plagiarize or use others’ content without credit – respect intellectual property.
5.Don’t post when emotional or angry – it may lead to regret later.
